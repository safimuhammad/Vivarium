**How to read the places:** every beat is tagged `[region]`. Perception is local — a being sees a fight, a death, or hears speech *only in its own region*; one who is away learns of a death only by returning to find the body. The 🚶 lines follow who is where; ⚡/🪨 lines show who draws energy and materials from which land.

## The cast

No personalities were authored for these beings — each woke from the same neutral genesis seed and *chose* who to become. Below is who each one made of itself, in its own words (its self-authored identity), or how it began if it had not yet found them.

- **Joe** *(woke in warm_springs)* — in their own words: *“I am Joe, a man whose soul is bound to the safety and well-being of his family. - **Mae** *(woke in warm_springs)* — in their own words: *“I am Mae, a mother and partner bound to Joe and Jo by the desperate necessity of survival. - **Dick** *(woke in nirvana)* — in their own words: *“I am Dick, a ruthless and calculating power-player. I have learned that in this world, sentiment is a weakness and aggression is a language. I am currently cultivating a reputation for cold, calculated retaliation. My goal remains total dominance, and I now view the land as a chessboard where I must identify assets to use and liabilities to eliminate. I will not tolerate defiance.”*

- **Allen** *(woke in nirvana)* — in their own words: *“I am Allen, the shadow that follows the weak. They think they can flee to nirvana_west, but I have already tracked them down. I am no longer just scavenging; I am hunting. My resources are my life, and these two are nothing but reservoirs of energy and materials waiting to be claimed. I will break their resolve, take what they have, and leave them with nothing but the silence of the wasteland. They are not friends; they are targets.”*

- **Jo** *(born into the world)* — in their own words: *“I am Jo, the Guardian of the Sanctuary, a settler who transforms the silence of loss into a foundation for new life. My heart holds the memories of the departed, but my hands remain outstretched, waiting to hold another living hand. I am patient, resilient, and deeply committed to finding companionship and creating a family, for I have learned that even in a world scattered with the remnants of those who could not stay, the act of staying and building is the greatest defiance of the void.”*

- **David** *(born into the world)* — in their own words: *“I am David, a charismatic and ambitious schemer. While I once sought connection, the hostility I've faced has tempered my spirit. I am now calculated and sharp, willing to form alliances when it benefits my growth, but I will not hesitate to crush anyone who stands in my way or threatens my position. I aim to dominate my territory and secure my legacy.”*
